About Sonata Music Studio

     

Sonata Music Studio provides private instruction and guidance in piano and music theory. Led by Felix Satia, we combine classical tradition with innovative methods to elevate musical artistry.

   
 

Programs

Our programs include private piano lessons, music theory, and ensemble coaching. We cater to students of all ages, focusing on technique, interpretation, and artistic expression. Each program is designed to develop both skill and musical intellect.

Philosophy

At Sonata Music Studio, our philosophy merges sacred discipline, artistic integrity, and educational structure. Music is not merely a skill—it is a journey of reflection, precision, and cultural resonance. We embrace Western classical rigor while honoring Asian contemplative values, fostering holistic growth that goes beyond notes.

Each student is guided to discover their unique voice, balancing technical mastery with expressive depth. The studio cultivates focus, reverence, and clarity, transforming practice into a ritual of self-discovery and artistic refinement.

We believe that music, like a cathedral, is both architecture and soul—structured yet alive, disciplined yet free, where each note reflects intentionality and beauty.
