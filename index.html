About

Sonata Music Studio is a sanctuary for structured musical growth rooted in cultural richness and reverence. Led by Felix Satia—music instructor, arranger, and composer—the studio combines classical precision with contemplative artistry, inspired by heritage, sacred tradition, and cross-cultural refinement.

Programs

  • Piano – Classical Repertoire & Technique
  • Music Theory – ABRSM, Trinity & Internal Curriculum
  • Choral/Vocal Coaching – Liturgical & Ensemble

Philosophy

Our philosophy is rooted in sacred discipline, artistry, and educational structure. Learning is not merely technical—it is spiritual, cultural, and transformative. We draw from Western classical training and Asian reflective values to create a holistic approach that transcends notes and embraces meaning.

Portfolio

Felix Satia holds a Bachelor of Music in Piano Performance and has presented repertoire including WTC by Bach, Beethoven Sonatas, Debussy Etudes, and Brahms Rhapsodies. He is also experienced in liturgical music leadership and theory instruction for institutional programs.
