About Felix Satia

Felix Satia is a professional piano and music theory instructor with years of experience in guiding students toward musical mastery. His approach emphasizes individual expression, precision, and integrating classical rigor with modern sensibilities. In each session, students explore harmony, rhythm, and phrasing in a way that nurtures creativity while solidifying technical skill. Beyond lessons, Felix inspires students to understand music as a living, breathing art form — a bridge between intellect, emotion, and spirituality. The studio space mirrors this philosophy: minimalistic elegance, subtle lighting, natural office vibe, and a serene ambiance conducive to deep focus and artistic reflection.

Programs

Private piano instruction, music theory workshops, ensemble coaching, and composition guidance. Each program is carefully structured, allowing flexible scheduling and personalized goal setting. Students are encouraged to explore diverse repertoire, from classical masterpieces to contemporary compositions, with guidance that balances technical challenge and expressive freedom. Detailed progress tracking and regular performance opportunities ensure continuous growth and tangible achievements.

Philosophy

Felix’s teaching philosophy is grounded in understanding music at its core: the interplay of structure and emotion, precision and soul. Lessons integrate cognitive engagement, physical technique, and artistic intuition. Emphasis is placed on reading, memorization, interpretation, and improvisation — cultivating musicians capable of both disciplined performance and inspired creativity. Students are inspired to see music as a reflection of life itself: layered, dynamic, and profound.

Portfolio

Featuring past student recitals, original compositions, collaborative projects, and curated performances. Each portfolio entry showcases artistic development, interpretative skill, and technical accomplishment. Stories behind the music are shared to highlight creative decisions, challenges overcome, and insights gained. The portfolio demonstrates a journey from novice to accomplished musician under structured guidance and mentorship.
