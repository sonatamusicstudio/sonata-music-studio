About

Sonata Music Studio is a sanctuary for structured musical growth rooted in cultural richness and reverence. Led by Felix Satia—music instructor, arranger, and composer—the studio combines classical precision with contemplative artistry, inspired by heritage, sacred tradition, and cross-cultural refinement.

Our mission is to cultivate artistic expression with precision, nurturing both technical skill and soulful interpretation. Students experience a holistic journey: disciplined training, deep understanding, and creative exploration.

Programs
  • Piano – Classical Repertoire & Technique
  • Music Theory – ABRSM, Trinity & Internal Curriculum
  • Choral/Vocal Coaching – Liturgical & Ensemble

Each program is meticulously designed to combine rigorous technique with artistic refinement. Lessons emphasize clarity, musicality, and personal growth, with attention to each student's unique abilities.

Philosophy

At Sonata Music Studio, our philosophy merges sacred discipline, artistic integrity, and educational structure. Music is not merely a skill—it is a journey of reflection, precision, and cultural resonance. We embrace Western classical rigor while honoring Asian contemplative values, fostering holistic growth that goes beyond notes.

Each student is guided to discover their unique voice, balancing technical mastery with expressive depth. The studio cultivates focus, reverence, and clarity, transforming practice into a ritual of self-discovery and artistic refinement.
