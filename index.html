About Felix Satia

Felix Satia is a dedicated piano and music theory instructor, merging classical precision with artistic vision. With years of experience teaching private piano, choral arrangements, and theory, he cultivates not only skill but a deep appreciation for the aesthetics of music. His approach blends rigorous training with contemplative artistry, creating a studio experience akin to a fusion of cathedral grandeur and museum-level refinement.

The studio philosophy emphasizes individual growth, intellectual curiosity, and expressive mastery, nurturing students to become confident performers and thoughtful musicians. Here, every note resonates with intention, and every lesson is an investment in lasting artistry.
