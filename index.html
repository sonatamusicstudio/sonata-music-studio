About Felix Satia

Felix Satia is a professional music instructor with years of experience in piano performance, music theory, and choral coaching. Combining artistic finesse with structured pedagogy, he guides students to achieve technical mastery and expressive musicality.

Programs

Piano Mastery

Private lessons designed for artistic growth and technical precision. Suitable for beginners to advanced students.

Music Theory

Structured approach to reading, analysis, and composition. Understand music from a conceptual and practical perspective.

Choral & Vocal Coaching

Guidance for choir members, soloists, and ensembles. Focus on expressive performance, intonation, and breath control.

Teaching Philosophy

Felix combines structured pedagogy with an appreciation for artistic expression. His approach emphasizes memory, interpretation, and personal growth, encouraging each student to develop both skill and individuality.
